December 1st, 2016

Good evening guys! Here is the new version of my Dart Counter!

There are some new features coming with the new counter: First it's now possible to play alone or to play with more than two players. Furthermore you can decide whether you want to play until all players have finished or restart the game. That's the next feature: You could restart the game with the same settings or you can change your settings. The last feature is a table which shows the results of the current game.

You have to enter all names of the players in the settings and you can choose whether you want to play 501 (default) or 301. It's possible to play with Double-Out (default) and Single-Out - feel free to click the buttons for each setting.

As introduced in the last version there are buttons with numbers for touch-devices like tablets or smartphones - the design is responsive which makes the counter looking good on mobile devices, too.

I'm thinking of introducing more statistics and tips for the players what they have to score to finish the game in the next version which I will probably publish next year.

Now you're ready for playing darts! Game on!

P.S.: I know that I've promised also a new design of this website - but I don't have much time at the moment so you have to wait a bit longer for that! Sorry!

August 7th, 2016

Hi everybody, I've just published the first version of my Dart Counter.

It's a counter for two players. You can decide whether you want to play with single or double out. It's also possible to choose between playing 301 or 501. I will improve it at some point in the future, e.g. to play with more players but I have no time for further development at the moment.

To choose your settings you just have to click the buttons. In the line above the players you can see the current settings. Default settings are 501 with double out. If you finished a game you can start a new one by clicking any button. Also you could enter your names if you want. The results of your previous throws can be seen under your current score.

You can enter any score from 0 to 180. If you have no score just click the button "Next Player!" or press your return key on the keyboard.
